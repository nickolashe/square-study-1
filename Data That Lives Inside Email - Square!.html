Data That Lives Inside Email - Square!

The Square IPO is upon us. Let’s take a look at some of the data that lives inside email!

Many businesses powered by Square offer to send you an email receipt upon completing the transaction. We used these receipts to understand customer purchasing behavior.

Most popular business locations

First let’s take a look at a few cities and the purchases we see over time.

Peak times of business

Maybe you’ve seen Google Maps show information about when a store is busiest. It is based on customers who opted-in to sharing location data. On the other hand, the purchase information extracted from email has real insight into the time of purchases and the amount of purchases. Here you can clearly see when a store is busy or not.

Let’s take Sightglass, one of Jack Dorsey’s own investments and one of the earliest Square locations. You can clearly see the purchase patterns throughout the day: a morning coffee rush just before work, a late morning cup of coffee and the afternoon pick me up.
